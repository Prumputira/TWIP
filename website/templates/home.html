True Wealth

The Anti-Consumerism Finance App

Introduction

We live in a world where consumerism dominates, urging us to spend more, own more, and chase fleeting satisfaction. But at "True Wealth," we believe that true prosperity is not found in possessions—it's found in purpose. Our app is built on the principle that financial freedom and mindful living go hand in hand. We are here to empower individuals to reclaim control over their money, align their spending with their values, and build a life of meaning beyond materialism.

Our Vision

To redefine wealth as a state of balance, intentionality, and fulfillment—where financial decisions support personal values, sustainable living, and long-term well-being.

Our Mission

To provide tools that inspire mindful financial choices, discourage overconsumption, and promote a culture of simplicity, sustainability, and empowerment.

Core Beliefs

Money Should Serve You

Money is a tool—not the goal. It should enable freedom, security, and joy rather than dictate your choices or trap you in cycles of debt and consumption.

Mindful Spending Matters

Every financial decision should reflect your values and priorities. By spending intentionally, you can achieve lasting fulfillment rather than momentary satisfaction.

Simplicity is Strength

A simpler life isn't about deprivation—it's about focusing on what truly matters. Reducing unnecessary consumption leads to greater clarity, peace of mind, and financial resilience.

Sustainability is Wealth

True wealth respects the planet and future generations. By consuming consciously and investing responsibly, we can contribute to a healthier world while securing our own future.

Empowerment Through Knowledge

Financial literacy is the foundation of freedom. Understanding your finances allows you to make informed decisions that align with your goals and values.

What We Stand For

Anti-Consumerism

We challenge the culture of excess by promoting thoughtful spending habits that prioritize needs over wants.

Transparency

We believe in clear financial insights that help you understand where your money goes—and why.

Community

We foster a supportive environment where users can share experiences, tips, and encouragement on their journey toward mindful living.

Accessibility

Our app is designed for everyone—whether you're just starting to budget or looking to refine your financial habits.

Our Approach

  • Zero-Based Budgeting: Every euro has a purpose—plan your spending to reflect what truly matters.
  • Bank Integration: Seamlessly track accounts, loans, subscriptions, and payments through secure connections powered by Tink.
  • Smart Insights: AI-driven tools help identify wasteful spending patterns and suggest ways to optimize your finances.
  • Sustainable Goals: Set long-term objectives that prioritize experiences, relationships, and ethical investments over material possessions.
  • Financial Pause Rule: Implement waiting periods for non-essential purchases to combat impulse spending.

The True Wealth Promise

We promise to guide you toward financial clarity and intentional living—not by telling you what you can't do but by empowering you with tools to make better choices. True wealth isn't about having more—it's about living better.

Join the Movement

Together, we can redefine wealth—not as an accumulation of things but as an abundance of purpose, freedom, and sustainability. With "True Wealth," take control of your finances and build a life that truly reflects who you are.

Let's create a world where mindful money leads to meaningful lives.
